# Bacon Cure Calculator

A simple bacon curing calculator that uses a master cure recipe and calculates ingredient amounts based on pork belly weight.

## Features

- Enter pork belly weight in pounds/ounces or grams
- Adjustable cure percentage (default: 3%)
- Editable master cure recipe
- Dark mode interface
- Calculates exact amounts of:
  - Salt
  - Sugar
  - Pink Salt (Cure #1)
  - Garlic
  - Black Pepper
  - Sage
  - Pepper Flakes

## Default Master Recipe

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|---------:|
| Salt | 400 g |
| Sugar | 200 g |
| Pink Salt | 60 g |
| Garlic | 15 g |
| Black Pepper | 15 g |
| Sage | 5 g |
| Pepper Flakes | 5 g |

## How It Works

1. Enter the weight of the pork belly.
2. Enter the desired cure percentage.
3. The calculator determines the total cure mix required.
4. The cure mix is split according to the master recipe proportions.

## Disclaimer

This tool is provided for educational and hobby use only. Always verify curing recipes and nitrite levels independently and follow food safety guidelines when curing meat.
